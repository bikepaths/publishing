## 3. Authoritative Lexicon

- **Dorsal-Vagal Shutdown**: A neurological state of conservation and immobilization triggered by chronic trauma and street danger. The prefrontal cortex is offline, making standard housing offers neurologically unreceivable.
- **Survival Friendship Network**: A network of verified bond partners on the street. Separating these partners at the housing threshold recreates the neurological threat state, destabilizing the transition.
- **Abundance Paradox**: The structural coexistence of extreme wealth and technology with severe unsheltered homelessness in the same physical geography.
