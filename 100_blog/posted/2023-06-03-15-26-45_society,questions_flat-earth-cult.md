Some people who believe the earth is flat display behaviors that resemble those of cult members. By rejecting scientific facts and ignoring proof, these individuals create a closed world. Although these individuals defend their alternative ideas with great energy, they do not belong to any formal or organized group. Under normal circumstances, holding a strange belief does not mean a person joins a cult.

Inside this community, prominent voices sometimes choose to change their minds. Mark Sargent became a famous advocate in documentaries before he publicly walked away in 2019. By applying careful critical thinking and studying verified scientific facts, he decided that his old views about the earth were wrong. In a similar way, a YouTube host named Patricia Steere left the movement in 2018.

Steere rejected the flat earth model after she noticed logical contradictions. After finding no proof for these claims, she chose to speak out and exit the group. By sharing these personal stories, former members show that individuals can escape rigid belief systems when they look at scientific evidence with a quiet and open mind. Through critical thinking, anyone can examine new information and rebuild a logical view of the world.
