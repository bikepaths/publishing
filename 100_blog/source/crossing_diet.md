## 1. Macro-Nutrition without Sugar or Nitrates

* **Wild-Caught Pink Salmon or Yellowfin Tuna Pouches**: Trade dense metal cans for vacuum-sealed foil pouches. Choose clean brands such as Wild Planet or Safe Catch. They contain only fish and sea salt, containing no inflammatory soy oil, broth fillers, or hidden nitrates. This provides highly concentrated, easily digestible lean protein to stop muscle breakdown without delaying stomach emptying.
* **Organic Sprouted Spelt or Whole Wheat Matzo**: Standard crackers contain hydrogenated palm oils and high-fructose corn syrup, both of which trigger transient LES valve relaxations. High-quality matzo contains exactly two ingredients, flour and water. It provides an immediate, ultra-light dry carbohydrate source to replenish muscle glycogen.
* **Gluten-Free Organic Sprouted Rolled Oats**: Instead of standard instant oatmeal packets loaded with chemical thickeners and refined sugar, buy pure sprouted oats. When mixed into your warm bicycle bottle water, the oats release natural beta-glucans. This forms a slick, soothing demulcent matrix that lines the esophagus and mechanically buffers stomach acid.
