# 04_CASE_STUDY_OUTLINE: Marcus Narrative Pipeline

Case study tracking client transition from Glendale Narrows riverbed encampment mapping to permanent tower graduation.

## Stage 1: Encampment Mapping (Registry)
*   **Subject Profile**: Marcus.
*   **Field Location**: Los Angeles Riverbed, Glendale Narrows, dense willow grove island adjacent to Bowtie Parcel (34.1165° N, 118.2615° W).
*   **Baseline Data Logs**: Physical description, approximate age, GPS coordinates.
*   **Survival Anchors**: Dog named Buster, double-tarped cart structure.
*   **Initial Classification**: Pipeline C (behaviorally calcified chronic, severe service refusal history).

## Stage 2: ACT Engagement (Trust Brokerage)
*   **Operational Tactics**: PSS establishes weekly presence. Deliver coffee, dog food, socks, wound dressings. Request zero paperwork. Require zero behavioral conditions.
*   **Neurological Goal**: Relational predictability to reduce dorsal-vagal threat reflexes.
*   **Data Updates**: Document behavioral transitions and somatic vulnerabilities in MDI HMIS registry.

## Stage 3: Crisis Window & Specific Offer
*   **Trigger Event**: Seasonal storm surge floods riverbed island; Buster presents acute respiratory distress.
*   **Warm Offer**: PSS delivers concrete offer: "Tower 1, Floor 12, Pod 2, Unit 12-04. Buster has veterinary suite 12-B three doors away."
*   **Bureaucracy Elimination**: Transport matches pre-completed social work file.

## Stage 4: Sally Port Handoff & Ground Floor Intake
*   **Arrival Node**: Transport routes to Zone A Sally Port.
*   **Kinetic Triage**: Somatic triage by registered nurse and PSS. Assess hypothermia and foot ulcerations.
*   **Hygiene Node**: Transfer to single-occupancy module. Warm water (38°C). Soiled clothes bagged and tracked.

## Stage 5: Phase Zero Metabolic Stabilization
*   **Hours 0–4**: Electrolyte rehydration (WHO ORS) to prevent hyponatremia.
*   **Hours 4–24**: Refeeding syndrome screening. Route to low-calorie reintroduction diet.
*   **Hours 24–72**: Somatic wound care; establish baseline psychiatric evaluation.
*   **Days 3–60**: Sleep restoration in soundproof Phase Zero Decompression module (STC 65). Circadian rhythm tracking.

## Stage 6: Pod Integration & Relational Recovery
*   **Vertical Move**: Transition to residential Pod Twelve (150-resident Dunbar limit).
*   **Relational Scaffolding**: PSS introduces Marcus to immediate suite neighbors.
*   **Shared Environment**: Transition from isolated room to communal kitchen and biophilic commons.

## Stage 7: Civic Agency & Pod Governance
*   **Cooperative Duty**: Marcus assumes kitchen maintenance shifts.
*   **Council Participation**: Weekly attendance at pod councils. Vote on floor rules and shared space layouts.
*   **Neurological Shift**: Cooperative governance reactivates prefrontal cortex executive pathways.

## Stage 8: Vocational Node & Financial Transition
*   **Tower Roles**: Marcus volunteers for robotic maintenance supervision.
*   **Benefit Alignment**: Finalize Medi-Cal billing and SSI restoration.
*   **Community Expansion**: Families or external advocates contacted via PSS mediation.

## Stage 9: Independent Graduation & Long-Term Stability
*   **Outplacement**: Transition to off-site permanent supportive housing.
*   **Relational Safety Net**: 90-day post-graduation ACT follow-up.
*   **Fiscal Audit**: Verify avoided municipal emergency service costs.
