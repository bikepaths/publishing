# 09_PHASE_ONE_OSINT_RESULTS: Field and Fiscal Integration

Results from Phase 1 OSINT execution verifying administrative structures, counting methodology, and clinical billing codes.

## 1. Administrative Oversight Transition
*   **Establishment of HSH**: Effective January 1, 2026, Los Angeles County centralized all homelessness operations under the newly created Department of Homeless Services & Housing (HSH).
*   **Book 4 Application**: Marcus's field registry and escalation paths will reference HSH monitors and compliance coordinators rather than fragmented legacy departments.

## 2. Riverbed Point-In-Time (PIT) Methodology
*   **Specialized Deployment**: Standard volunteer tallies are prohibited in riverbeds due to physical hazards. Only professional, multi-disciplinary outreach teams are deployed to hard-to-reach sectors (canopies, concrete channels).
*   **Undercount Bias Factors**:
    *   *Seasonal Dispersal*: Wet weather shifts encampment coordinates.
    *   *Tactical Evasion*: Camps are intentionally obscured to avoid municipal sweeps.
*   **Book 4 Application**: Stage 1 mapping incorporates professional outreach team logs and addresses the gap between official PIT tallies and real-world occupancy metrics.

## 3. Street Medicine and Clinical Billing Codes
*   **Place of Service (POS) Codes**:
    *   **POS 27 (Outreach Site/Street)**: Primary code for billing wound care, diagnostic evaluations, and psychiatric interventions in unsheltered encampments.
    *   **POS 15 (Mobile Unit)**: Primary code for services delivered within ACT mobile medical vans.
    *   **POS 04 (Homeless Shelter)** / **POS 16 (Temporary Lodging)**: Supporting codes for Phase Zero stabilization.
*   **CalAIM Integration**:
    *   **Direct Access Contracting**: Allows street medicine providers to bypass Primary Care Provider (PCP) assignments. Allows immediate billing regardless of client's official care network lock.
    *   **Enhanced Care Management (ECM)**: Funding mechanism for pod integration stewards and caseworkers.
*   **Book 4 Application**: Refines Phase Zero financial and cost-avoidance audits by utilizing exact POS 27 billing trajectories for cellulitis treatment.
