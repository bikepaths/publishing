# 05_CHAPTER_OUTLINE: Book Four Narrative Outline

Detailed twelve-chapter trajectory mapping client transition from riverbed isolation to independent graduation.

## Chapter 1: The Invisible Island
*   **Narrative Focus**: Introduce Marcus and dog Buster living on Glendale Narrows riverbed island.
*   **Operational Concept**: Encampment mapping (Stage 1). Field Registry teams catalog GPS coordinates, survival anchors (Buster, double-tarped structure), and initial Pipeline C status.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Survival exoskeleton and sensory hyper-vigilance.

## Chapter 2: The Art of Presence
*   **Narrative Focus**: PSS and ACT outreach team initiate low-barrier contact.
*   **Operational Concept**: Outreach protocols (Stage 2). Predictable schedules, presence-first approach. Deliver dog food, dry socks, and coffee. Request zero paperwork or intake requirements.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Polyvagal theory and de-escalation of threat neuroception.

## Chapter 3: The Storm Surge
*   **Narrative Focus**: Winter storm surge floods Glendale Narrows. Buster develops acute respiratory distress.
*   **Operational Concept**: Crisis window and specific warm offer (Stage 3). PSS presents immediate, concrete unit match and veterinary placement.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Flash-flood exposure and acute panic response.

## Chapter 4: The Sally Port Gate
*   **Narrative Focus**: Transport and arrival at Tower One.
*   **Operational Concept**: Ground floor kinetic triage (Stage 4). Zone A Sally Port arrival, circadian lighting mitigation, and 38°C single-occupancy hygiene nodes. Preserving personal assets.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Sensory overload containment.

## Chapter 5: Rehydration and Refeeding
*   **Narrative Focus**: Marcus's first 72 hours of clinical stabilization.
*   **Operational Concept**: Metabolic sequencing (Stage 5). WHO ORS rehydration, nutritional refeeding protocol to prevent refeeding syndrome, and debridement of bilateral immersion foot.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Somatic shock prevention.

## Chapter 6: Adrenal Decompression
*   **Narrative Focus**: Sixty days in Phase Zero Decompression module.
*   **Operational Concept**: Rest-driven neurological recovery (Stage 5). STC 65 soundproof module residency, salivary cortisol tracking, and restoration of circadian sleep.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Sympathetic nervous system overdrive remission.

## Chapter 7: Entering Pod Twelve
*   **Narrative Focus**: Transition from Phase Zero ground floor to residential levels.
*   **Operational Concept**: Dunbar-scale relational scaffolding (Stage 6). Transition to 150-resident Pod Twelve. Introduction to live-in Pod Steward and six-unit suite cohort.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Social engagement system reactivation.

## Chapter 8: Pod Governance
*   **Narrative Focus**: Marcus attends floor council meetings.
*   **Operational Concept**: Civic agency and pod councils (Stage 7). Group decision-making, floor rule votes, and conflict co-regulation.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Prefrontal cortex pathway reconstitution.

## Chapter 9: The Cafe Stewardship
*   **Narrative Focus**: Marcus assumes operational responsibility.
*   **Operational Concept**: Vocational integration (Stage 8). Appointment to Assistant Cafe Steward, coordinating robotic kitchen inventory tracking.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Self-efficacy and occupational agency.

## Chapter 10: Clinical Integration
*   **Narrative Focus**: Financial and systemic stabilization.
*   **Operational Concept**: Administrative alignment (Stage 8). Medi-Cal registration, SSI restoration, and veterinary care tracking for Buster.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Administrative safety and resource predictability.

## Chapter 11: The Graduation Protocol
*   **Narrative Focus**: Transition out of Tower One.
*   **Operational Concept**: Outplacement protocol (Stage 9). Move to off-site permanent supportive housing, initiating 90-day transitional ACT follow-up.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Autonomous stability maintenance.

## Chapter 12: The Municipal Yield
*   **Narrative Focus**: Fiscal audit of Marcus's transition. County HSH monitors demand immediate performance metrics amid 2026 budget contractions, threatening to defund Tower One pilot if recidivism rates match standard 46% control levels.
*   **Operational Concept**: Cost-avoidance analysis (Stage 9). Detail avoided emergency transports, psychiatric holds, and rescue operations. Present Marcus's 10-month stabilization as clinical proof of MDI cost-efficiency to secure ongoing CalAIM funding.
*   **Somatic Theme**: Economic sustainability of material dignity systems.
