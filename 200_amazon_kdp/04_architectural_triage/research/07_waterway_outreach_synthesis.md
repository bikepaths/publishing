# 07_WATERWAY_OUTREACH_SYNTHESIS: Field Report Analysis

Synthesis of media reports and official county data validating MDI waterway outreach protocols.

## 1. Documented Source Baselines
*   **Source A**: New York Post, March 29, 2026 ("LA's homeless crisis hits shocking new low").
*   **Source B**: LA County Homeless Initiative, October 2025 ("A Pathway Home in Whittier Narrows").

## 2. Validation of Case Study Parameters

### A. Somatic Exhaustion and Physical Trauma
*   **County Data (Source B)**: Ernesto (age 65) presenting chronic knee arthritis, making riverbed navigation physically prohibitive.
*   **MDI Alignment**: Validates Stage 4 & 5 prioritization of physical triage, mobility restoration, and immediate clinical intervention.

### B. Animal Companion Anchor Mechanics
*   **County Data (Source B)**: Jonathan and Liliana (cats LT and Elvis) and Hernan (French bulldog Frenchie) refusing standard moves until pet accommodation and pet food delivery established.
*   **MDI Alignment**: Validates Marcus-Buster non-separation protocol. Trust brokerage (Stage 2) requires delivering pet supplies. Transition (Stage 3) requires pet-friendly housing matches.

### C. Therapeutic Environment and Nature Attachment
*   **County Data (Source B)**: Hernan utilizing riverbed nature to heal from trauma, refusing indoor housing due to sterile configurations.
*   **MDI Alignment**: Validates Phase Zero biophilic design commons. Pod architecture must incorporate organic elements to replace outdoor safety anchors.

### D. Long-Term Outreach Timelines
*   **County Data (Source B)**: HOST Deputy Carlos engaging client for three years before transition agreement. LAHSA outreach worker Kiowa Torkay emphasizing timing and relational consistency.
*   **MDI Alignment**: Validates Stage 2 duration. Trust building requires predictable presence over months/years.

### E. Environmental and Hydrological Risks
*   **Media Report (Source A)**: Exposure to toxic runoff (bathing in river water) and high risk of entrapment during flash-flood surges.
*   **MDI Alignment**: Validates Stage 3 trigger (seasonal storm surge) and Stage 1 environmental hazard mapping.
