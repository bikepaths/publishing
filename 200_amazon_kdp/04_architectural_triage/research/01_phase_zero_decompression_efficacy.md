# Phase Zero Decompression Efficacy: Marcus

Clinical documentation of metabolic stabilization and neurological decompression.

*   **Extraction Trigger**: Riverbed flash flood surge during winter rainstorm. Emergency extraction by ACT team and Peer Support Specialists.
*   **Arrival Channel**: Zone A Sally Port. Interlocking doors maintained secure, low-stimulation environment.
*   **Initial Engagement**: Low-barrier hygiene node. Shower water temperature locked at 38°C. Clothes preserved in identity-saving bags.

## 2. Metabolic Stabilization Sequence
*   **Hours 0–4 (Rehydration)**: WHO-formulated electrolyte solution administered orally. Avoided plain water loading to prevent cerebral edema.
*   **Hours 4–24 (Nutritional Triage)**: Triage identified BMI under 16. Initiated low-calorie, low-phosphate nutrition. Prevented cardiac arrhythmias from Refeeding Syndrome.
*   **Hours 24–72 (Clinical Care)**: Debridement of bilateral immersion foot macerations. Administered wound dressings and broad-spectrum antibiotics. Non-invasive psychiatric evaluation initiated after metabolic baseline stabilized.

## 3. Neurological Decompression (Days 3–60)
*   **Housing Unit**: Housed in soundproof Phase Zero Decompression module (STC 65 rating). Secure, resident-only lock control.
*   **Endocrine Monitoring**: Salivary cortisol tracking showed 72% reduction by Day 60.
*   **Vigilance Remission**: Shifted from dorsal-vagal shutdown to active parasympathetic recovery. Circadian sleep cycles restored to 7.5 contiguous hours. Immersion foot cellulitis resolved completely.
