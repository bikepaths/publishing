## SECTION 4: KEY THEORETICAL CONCEPTS (MDI-A LaTeX)

**The Kinship Deficit:** Post-war nuclear family dissolution destroyed the organic kinship network — the zero-interest economic shock absorber historically buffering individuals against market displacement.

**The State Trauma Engine:** The municipal intervention apparatus systematically destroys ontological security. It demands behavioral compliance from populations whose neurological capacity for compliance has been destroyed by the intervention process itself.

**Ontological Security (Giddens 1991):** Four markers: environmental constancy, daily routines, spatial control, secure base for identity. The street destroys all four simultaneously. The MDI tower restores all four simultaneously.

**The Synthetic Urban Commons:** MDI engineers the functional equivalent of dissolved organic commons — kinship network, religious congregation, extended household — at industrial scale.

**The Utility Model:** Shelter, hygiene, thermal safety, and privacy reclassified as public industrial consumables delivered at cost. Not welfare. Not charity. Infrastructure.

**Four Core Principles:**
1. Metabolic Stabilization — physiological recovery before placement
2. Ontological Security — acoustic and spatial privacy

4. The Utility Model — shelter as public consumable
