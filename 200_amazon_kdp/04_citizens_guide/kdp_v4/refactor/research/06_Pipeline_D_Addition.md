# Pre-Compositional Research: Pipeline D Addition

## 1. Harm Reduction Services Available
For Pipeline D individuals—those who retain executive capacity but refuse residential placement—MDI shifts from a containment model to a strict harm reduction and stabilization model in the field. 
*   **Medical and Somatic Care:** Access to mobile street medicine teams for wound care, infection control, and chronic disease management without the prerequisite of sobriety or shelter compliance.
*   **Biological Needs:** Provision of high-grade survival gear, hydration, and nutritional support to maintain basic physiological integrity (Nussbaum Capabilities 1 and 2: Life and Bodily Health).
*   **Safe Consumption:** Where legally permissible, integration with supervised consumption sites and immediate distribution of overdose reversal antagonists (naloxone).

## 2. Maintenance of the Standing Offer
The core philosophy of Pipeline D management is that refusal of service is not a permanent state; it is a snapshot of current trauma or addiction severity.
*   **The Unconditional Offer:** The offer for immediate entry into Phase Zero is permanently standing. It is never revoked due to past non-compliance, behavioral outbursts, or relapse.
*   **Communication Mechanism:** Mobile outreach teams consistently communicate the availability of the Dunbar Pods. The message is stripped of moralizing or mandates: "Your room is waiting when you are ready. It will be quiet, safe, and yours." This shifts the psychological burden from the system attempting to coerce the individual, to the individual retaining agency over their entry.

## 3. The Gardien Pathway
The "Gardien" (Guardian) pathway represents MDI's specialized non-residential outreach protocol.
*   **Sustained Engagement:** Gardiens are assigned specific geographic zones to build longitudinal trust with Pipeline D individuals over months or years. They do not operate as enforcement; they operate as consistent, reliable stewards.
*   **Without Requiring Placement:** Gardiens facilitate access to off-site hygiene centers, secure storage for belongings, and legal/identity documentation retrieval. They act as the individual's proxy to the formal system, navigating bureaucratic friction so the individual does not have to. 

## 4. Connection to Nussbaum's Remaining Capabilities
Even without entering a Dunbar Pod, Pipeline D individuals must be granted access to the higher-order capabilities through MDI's expanded civic footprint:
*   **Affiliation (Capability 7) & Play (Capability 9):** Gardiens facilitate access to safe, decentralized community drop-in centers where individuals can engage in low-stakes social interaction, share meals, and experience recreation without the pressure of case management or behavioral mandates.
*   **Other Species (Capability 8):** Street outreach teams provide veterinary care, food, and protective resources for the companion animals of Pipeline D individuals, recognizing that these animals are often their primary source of emotional attachment and security.
*   **Practical Reason (Capability 6):** By providing secure storage and stabilizing basic biological needs on the street, MDI frees up the individual's cognitive bandwidth. This allows them the space to conceptualize their own vision of a good life and plan for the future, ultimately fostering the internal motivation necessary to eventually accept the standing offer for Phase Zero.
