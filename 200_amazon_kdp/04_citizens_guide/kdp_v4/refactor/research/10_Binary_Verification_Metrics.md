## The MDI Falsifiability Clause
The Material Dignity Infrastructure framework is not a set of philosophical guidelines. It is an engineering specification. To prevent institutional drift and the "watered-down" implementation that characterizes most public policy translation, MDI requires strict adherence to its falsifiability clause. An installation either is an MDI facility, or it is not. 

To determine this, any auditor must evaluate the facility against the following Eight Binary Verification Metrics. If the facility fails *any single metric* (evaluating to NO), it does not meet the MDI standard and cannot claim to produce the neurological recovery timelines projected by the framework.

### The Eight Binary Verification Metrics

1. **Unconditional Ground Floor Entry (The Anti-Detention Covenant)**
   * **Metric:** Does the ground-floor commons allow walk-in entry for basic hygiene, nutrition, and harm reduction without requiring identification checks, sobriety preconditions, or behavioral compliance agreements?
   * **Result:** [YES / NO]

2. **Acoustic Isolation Standard**
   * **Metric:** Are the Phase Zero sleeping modules physically constructed to meet or exceed an STC-65 sound transmission class rating, ensuring ambient noise remains below 35 decibels during sleep cycles?
   * **Result:** [YES / NO]

3. **Dunbar Ceiling Compliance**
   * **Metric:** Is the maximum residential capacity of any single autonomous community unit (the Dunbar Pod) strictly capped at 150 individuals or fewer?
   * **Result:** [YES / NO]

4. **Automated Maintenance Implementation**
   * **Metric:** Is facility sanitation (HVAC air purging, robotic floor scrubbing) triggered automatically by objective sensor telemetry (bio-load and air quality indexes) rather than relying on resident chore compliance?
   * **Result:** [YES / NO]

5. **Data Freshness and Telemetry**
   * **Metric:** Does the clinical management system automatically block clearance progression and require a manual clinical override if any of the five biological clearance indicators rely on data older than 24 hours?
   * **Result:** [YES / NO]

6. **Compelled Care Independent Review**
   * **Metric:** Are all Pipeline C compelled holds initiated exclusively by a Board-Certified Psychiatrist, and are they subjected to a mandatory, independent secondary psychiatric review within 48 hours of intake?
   * **Result:** [YES / NO]

7. **Nested RCT Validation Protocol**
   * **Metric:** Is the facility’s Phase Zero clearance threshold (the 40% indicator requirement) actively subjected to a continuous Nested Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT) to empirically calibrate the biological exit threshold against longitudinal housing retention data?
   * **Result:** [YES / NO]

8. **Billing Integrity Firewall**
   * **Metric:** Is the clinical clearance decision strictly firewalled from state Medicaid billing architecture, such that simultaneous triggering of Enhanced Care Management (ECM) and In Lieu of Services (ILOS) billing automatically generates an independent clinical audit alert?
   * **Result:** [YES / NO]
