# Chapter 25: Comparative Models

Material Dignity Infrastructure does not represent the only proposal addressing chronic homelessness. Policy makers seriously advance four alternative infrastructure models. Each model possesses genuine strengths. Each model also contains a specific structural failure mode rendering it entirely insufficient for the Pipeline C cohort. 

Autonomous Mobile Fleet Deployment utilizes heavy industrial vehicles to provide mobile clinical services. The model offers rapid deployment capabilities and low regulatory friction. The failure mode occurs at the biological level. Mobile fleets provide zero permanent residential stability. The system cannot execute a Phase Zero intake. A brain returning to the street every night cannot achieve the continuous sleep required for cortisol reduction. 

Tax-Credit Decentralized Micro-Zoning constructs small scattered-site developments across multiple neighborhoods. This model effectively distributes neighborhood political opposition. The failure mode occurs at the logistical level. The model lacks the clinical concentration required for efficient medical delivery. Furthermore, it replicates the sovereign isolation failure. Placing profoundly traumatized individuals into isolated apartments guarantees social tracking failure and clinical abandonment. 

Universal Basic Infrastructure Vouchers provide a demand-side subsidy allowing individuals to purchase their own stabilization services. This model maximizes individual agency and market flexibility. The failure mode occurs at the cognitive level. The system demands that an individual navigate a complex administrative market. A person experiencing severe anosognosia completely lacks the cognitive capacity to execute this navigation. The model demands the exact executive function that the crisis has destroyed. 

Standalone Automated Office-to-Pod Conversion retrofits commercial towers into residential pods without integrating clinical services. This model offers rapid physical conversion at a lower initial cost than a complete MDI installation. The failure mode occurs at the triage level. The model places neurologically damaged individuals directly into independent units without executing the Phase Zero metabolic stabilization layer. The physical building exists. The clinical infrastructure does not. Placing damaged brains into a building does not constitute medical stabilization. 

Integrated Material Dignity Infrastructure succeeds exactly where these partial models fail. MDI synchronizes physical architectural conversion, strict biological triage, and structured pod governance into a single operational unit. It addresses the neurological damage directly rather than merely addressing the superficial lack of a physical address.
