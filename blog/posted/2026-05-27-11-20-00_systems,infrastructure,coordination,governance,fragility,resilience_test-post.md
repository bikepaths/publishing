**The Infrastructure of Systems Coordination**

Systems coordination requires structural design to maintain homeostatic equilibrium. When we build synthetic commons structures, we place redundant nodes within the network layout. Three redundant power nodes provide the necessary safety margin to prevent coordination friction during system stress.

#### Glossary
*Homeostatic Equilibrium* Systemic balance maintained through coordination loops.

#### Assumptions and Assertions
1. Redundancy reduces coordination friction.
2. The synthetic commons requires 3 redundant power nodes.
