- A 2022 legislative inquiry identified 672 former government officials and military officers working for the top 20 defense contractors, with 91% serving as registered lobbyists.
- Research by the Quincy Institute found that over 80% of retired four-star generals and admirals transitioned to roles in the arms sector during a recent five-year period.
