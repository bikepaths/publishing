# Manual of Style: KDP General Audience

## 1. Core Voice: The Empirical Witness

The narrative voice is an active witness who has observed street conditions firsthand. 
- State observations directly using sensory details from the physical landscape.
- Do not hide behind academic detachment or clinical distance; use the authority of direct experience.
- Maintain a tone of serious witness rather than moral scolding or partisan advocacy.

## 2. Syntax and Readability: Direct and Plain

The writing must be accessible to readers under high cognitive load.
- Target reading grade level: Grade 8.0 to Grade 10.0.
- Eliminate all academic clichés and jargon. 
- Map abstract systems to mechanical equivalents:
  - Translate "systemic dependency" to "subsidy loop" or "budget machine."
  - Translate "cognitive impairment" to "damaged wiring" or "biological ceiling."
- Rotate sentence openings: No more than two consecutive sentences may start with the subject noun.

## 3. Cadence and Rhythm: The Takeaway Hammer

Vary sentence length to build narrative momentum.
- Alternate descriptive passages detailing physical or biological mechanisms with short, declarative summaries.
- Use the Takeaway Hammer: Follow a complex explanation of a system or biology with a sudden, single-digit word declarative statement.
- Ensure sentence length variance: Consecutive sentences must not share identical word counts.

## 4. Punctuation Constraints

Maintain absolute clarity by restricting punctuation to simple structures.
- No em-dashes. Express parenthetical thoughts through syntax alone.
- No parentheses. Keep all descriptive content within the primary sentence structure; acronyms like HUD or CARE are authorized without parentheses after first definition.
- No colons as hard stops within sentences. Colons are authorized only to introduce formal lists or defining clauses.
- Link causal relationships using coordinate and subordinate conjunctions.

## 5. Structural Purity

Chapters must read as continuous narrative.
- Eliminate all subheadings, bullet points, and numbered lists within the main chapter text.
- Rely on prose movement to signal transitions between narrative, clinical, and economic sections.

## 6. Vocabulary Auditing (Anti-Slop Index)

Purge the following words from the manuscript:
- Adverbs and intensity crutches: actually, merely, simply, fundamentally, specifically, however, completely, absolute, perfectly, entirely, explicitly, active, smoothly, flawless, safely, secure, rigorously, violent, heavily, essentially, directly, certain, of course.
- Academic and transition filler: utilize, provenance, nuance, mitigate, synergy, paradigm, holistic, impactful, framework, leverage, facilitate, robust, seamless, comprehensive, vibrant, cutting-edge, state-of-the-art, game-changer, groundbreaking, revolutionary, transformative, foster, elevate, empower, ensure, stakeholder, actionable insights, best practices, furthermore, moreover, additionally, consequently, therefore, thus, hence, subsequently, in summary, in conclusion.
