# OVP Lite Specification for Web Posts

**Grade Level & Tone:**
- 8th-grade pedagogical English (Flesch-Kincaid 7.0-10.0).
- Tone is educational but not cold or clinical. Speak to a capable, present reader. 
- Use physical metaphors (e.g. soil, engines, foundations, circulation) to explain complex abstract systems.

**Chronology & Structure:**
- Narrative moves in a straight line: Observation first, Mechanism second, Implication third.
- Dynamic, unrestricted document length. Expand structurally as necessary to achieve deep conceptual synthesis.
- No #, ##, or ### headings. Only #### or bold headings allowed.
- Start EXACTLY with these three lines:
Title: [Insert Title]
Description: [Insert 1-sentence description]
Tags: [Pick EXACTLY 6 tags. The first tag MUST be from: money, society, skills, systems, nature, technology, adventure, health, history, mind]

**Strict Rules:**
- Define all clinical or complex terminology upon first use.
- Do NOT use: utilize, leverage, fosters, greatly, solely, nuanced, holistic, seamless, heavy, heavily, essential, fundamentally, specifically, perfectly, merely, further, however, moreover, additionally, furthermore, nevertheless.
- Do NOT use: "In conclusion", "In summary", "It is important to note that".
- Do NOT use em-dashes (—).
- Do NOT use synthetic contrast ("not X, but Y").
- Do NOT phoneticize numbers (e.g., use "2005", not "twenty oh five").
- Spell out numbers one through nine; use numerals for 10+.
