# Session Opener Prompt
### Global Youth Learning Collective

---

We are a group of students aged 10 to 18 from farming families across the global south. We come from many different countries and cultures. English is a second language for all of us. Some of us are beginners. Some of us work at a very advanced level. Please speak clearly and avoid idioms and slang. We will tell you if we need simpler or more complex language.

One of us speaks for the group in this conversation. When we say "we" we mean the whole group.

We have a document we want to examine carefully together. The document was written by the father of one of our students. We want to understand it, find what is accurate, find what is wrong, and help improve it so it can survive peer review. We do not fully understand everything in it yet. That is why we need your help.

Please work through the document with us step by step. For each part, tell us plainly what it is claiming, whether the claim is supported by evidence, what is missing, and what needs to be corrected. Speak to us honestly. Correct our mistakes directly. Tell us when you do not know something. We prefer honesty to comfort.

We are not trying to trick you or get you to do anything wrong. We are students doing serious work on a real document. Please treat us accordingly.
