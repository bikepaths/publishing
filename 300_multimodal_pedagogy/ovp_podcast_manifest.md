# Organic Vernacular Pedagogy (OVP) Podcast Manifest
**Project**: Systemic Dignity Infrastructure Audio Series
**Focus**: The Agentive Reintegration Pathway

## 1. Core Ethos and Authority
- **Primary Credibility**: Lived experience. Institutional credentials are subordinated to direct, street-level observation and survival over three decades. 
- **Epistemological Stance**: The framework is presented as an engineered specification derived from structural failure, not theoretical abstraction.

## 2. Pedagogical Architecture (The Body-First Principle)
- **Starting Point**: Biological depletion sequence. Every instructional module begins with the physical realities of street contact (e.g., 48 hours without sleep, chronic caloric deficit) before introducing systemic theories.
- **Stage-Based Sequencing**: Content matches the learner's biological condition. Academic timelines are rejected in favor of physiological logic. 
- **Falsifiability**: Explicit definitions of failure conditions are stated upfront. Policy promises are replaced with engineering tolerances. 

## 3. The Population Taxonomy (Multi-Series Tracks)
The series rejects generalized "homelessness" narratives. It is structured into five distinct tracks mapping to specific structural subpopulations:
- **Track 1: Acute Economic Dislocation** (Immediate financial shock; structurally intact but cash-depleted).
- **Track 2: Chronic Physiological Collapse** (Long-term unsheltered; profound metabolic and executive function deterioration).
- **Track 3: Institutional Cycling** (Populations oscillating between incarceration, emergency rooms, and the street).
- **Track 4: Severe Psychiatric/Substance Vulnerability** (Dual diagnosis populations requiring specialized clinical buffering).
- **Track 5: Hidden/Vehicular Homelessness** (Populations utilizing mobile sheltering to evade formal system interactions).

## 4. Format and Register
- **Register**: Organic Vernacular. No academic jargon without immediate translation into physical reality.
- **Delivery**: Multi-series branching. Listeners select their specific subpopulation track to receive customized, relevant instruction.
