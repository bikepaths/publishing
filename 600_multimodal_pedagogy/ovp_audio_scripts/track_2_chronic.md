# OVP Audio Track 2: Chronic Physiological Collapse

**Body-First Focus**: Chronic caloric deficit, severe sleep deprivation, environmental exposure (weather/violence).
**Core Message**: "Your body is eating itself. You cannot think your way out of starvation. You need caloric predictability and a secure door. Everything else is secondary."
**Systemic Concept Translation**: Phase Zero metabolic stabilization. We must stop the physiological collapse before demanding economic participation. Identity capital rebuilds slowly through internal cooperative labor, insulated from the external market.
