# OVP Audio Track 2: Chronic Physiological Collapse

**Target Audience**: Long-term unsheltered individuals with profound metabolic deterioration.
**Body-First Focus**: Chronic caloric deficit, severe sleep deprivation, environmental exposure.
**Core Message**: "Your body is eating itself. You cannot think your way out of starvation. You need caloric predictability and a secure door. Everything else is secondary."
**Systemic Concept Translation**: Phase Zero metabolic stabilization. We must stop the physiological collapse before demanding economic participation. 

---

## Episode 1 Dialogue Draft: The Physics of Starvation

**[Ambient Sound: Low, rhythmic heartbeat. No music. The tone is dead-level, exhausted, but authoritative.]**

**NARRATOR:**
If you've been on the concrete for more than a month, you already know what I'm about to say. But you probably haven't had the words for it. You think you're losing your mind. You think your willpower is gone. You're wrong.

Your body is eating itself. 

Right now, your prefrontal cortex—the part of your brain that makes long-term plans, the part that holds down a job, the part that navigates a housing application—is starved of glucose. When you don't sleep for three days because you have to keep one eye open, and when your only calories come from processed sugar at a gas station, your body shifts into sheer survival mechanics. 

You aren't making bad choices. You are making the only choices a starving, hunted animal can make. Immediate rewards. Immediate safety. Tomorrow does not exist when your biology thinks you might die tonight. 

**[Beat. One second of silence.]**

**NARRATOR:**
The system will tell you to get a job. The system will tell you to go to rehab. The system will hand you a list of numbers to call. 

Throw the list away.

You cannot think your way out of starvation. The first step—the *only* step—is metabolic stabilization. You need a door that locks from the inside. You need consecutive nights of REM sleep. You need predictable, heavy calories. Until you have that, any system demanding you act like a functional citizen is setting you up to fail. 

We call this Phase Zero. Before you can work, before you can heal, you have to stop dying.

## Episode 2 Dialogue Draft: The Return of Executive Function

**[Ambient Sound: The rhythmic hum of a commercial refrigerator. The sound of a door clicking shut.]**

**NARRATOR:**
You’ve been inside for two weeks. You’ve had predictable calories. You’ve had a locked door. 

For the first time in years, you slept for eight hours without waking up to defend yourself. 

Something strange is happening. The fog is lifting. You’re starting to think about tomorrow. You’re starting to feel bored. That boredom is the greatest victory you’ve had in a decade. Boredom means your body is no longer convinced it’s dying. 

But don't rush it. The external market is still toxic. Your identity capital is still depleted. 

Now we begin the internal work. Now we introduce the micro-economy. You don't need a resume here. You just need to show up and wash the dishes. We are going to rebuild your capacity for labor, slowly, safely, and entirely on your terms.
