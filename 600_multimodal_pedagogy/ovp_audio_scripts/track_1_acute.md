# OVP Audio Track 1: Acute Economic Dislocation
**Target Audience**: Structurally intact individuals experiencing immediate financial shock.
**Body-First Focus**: The physical panic of sudden displacement; adrenal fatigue and sleep disruption in the first 72 hours.
**Core Message**: "Your executive function is intact, but the shock is blinding you. Stop bleeding cash. Secure safe sleep immediately. Do not interact with the chronic system; you do not need it yet."
**Systemic Concept Translation**: The return deficit has not hit you yet. Preserve your professional references and resume continuity. Use micro-stabilization to bounce back before stigma attaches.
