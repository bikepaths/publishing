# OVP Audio Track 1: Acute Economic Dislocation

**Target Audience**: Structurally intact individuals experiencing immediate financial shock.
**Body-First Focus**: The physical panic of sudden displacement; adrenal fatigue and sleep disruption in the first 72 hours.
**Core Message**: "Your executive function is intact, but the shock is blinding you. Stop bleeding cash. Secure safe sleep immediately. Do not interact with the chronic system; you do not need it yet."
**Systemic Concept Translation**: The return deficit has not hit you yet. Preserve your professional references and resume continuity. Use micro-stabilization to bounce back before stigma attaches.

---

## Episode 1 Dialogue Draft: The Physics of Panic

**[Ambient Sound: Subtle, fast-paced ticking. The muffled sound of traffic through a closed car window.]**

**NARRATOR:**
You are sitting in your car. It’s 2:00 AM, and it is the first night you didn’t have a key to a front door. 

Your heart is beating fast. You are running through the math of your bank account over and over again, trying to figure out how a missed paycheck, a medical bill, and an eviction notice put you in a parking lot. 

Stop doing the math. 

Right now, you are experiencing the physics of panic. Your adrenal glands are flooding your system. You feel like you have to fix everything before the sun comes up. If you act on that panic, you will make decisions that accelerate your collapse. 

**[Beat. One second of silence.]**

**NARRATOR:**
Listen to me carefully: You are not chronic. Your mind is intact. You have a resume. You know how to operate in the normal economy. The structural return deficit hasn't attached its stigma to you yet. 

Your only job for the next 72 hours is to protect your remaining identity capital. 

Do not go to a congregate shelter. The chronic system is designed for populations experiencing physiological collapse. If you enter that environment tonight, the trauma and sleep deprivation will strip away the executive function you still have. You need to isolate. You need a safe parking program, a couch, or a micro-stabilization unit. 

Hold onto your phone. Keep your clothes clean. Sleep. The normal economy still recognizes you, but you have to stop bleeding cash and you have to stop panicking. We are going to build a firewall around you right now, before the street gets its hands on your biology.
