# Chapter 14: Phase Zero

When a person first comes off the street, they are not ready to live near other people. The body has spent months or years running on pure fear. The brain is still waiting for an attack. The old system ignores this fact. It takes an exhausted person and puts them right next to strangers in a loud shelter. This mistake causes fights and panic because the person’s body is still acting like it is on the sidewalk. Our project uses a different blueprint called Phase Zero.

Phase Zero works like an airlock on a spaceship. It is a physical zone that stands between the chaos of the street and the calm of the tower. When a person first arrives, they do not go straight to a normal floor. Instead, they enter a private room built strictly for deep rest. This room is a medical tool designed to break the cycle of fear. The most important part of this room is the silence. 

The walls of a Phase Zero room are built using a standard called STC 65. This is a builder's number that means total soundproofing. Behind a closed door, the person cannot hear police sirens, yelling, or heavy footsteps in the hall. For the first time in years, the brain realizes there is no danger. This deep silence forces the body to drop its guard. The person finally falls into the heavy sleep needed to fix their broken mind.

The city must provide food and medicine during this phase. The person does not need to worry about the rules of society because their only job is to sleep and eat. We call this a stabilization window. The body needs this time to rebuild its strength and pay back its massive energy debt. A person might stay in Phase Zero for a few days or a few weeks depending on how much damage they suffered on the street.

Only after the body is fixed can the next step begin. Once the brain is clear and the fear is gone, the person is ready to join a small community. They move out of the airlock and up to a normal floor with neighbors. By using this physical bridge, the tower protects both the new arrival and the rest of the building. The airlock makes sure that no one brings the chaos of the street inside the safe zone.
