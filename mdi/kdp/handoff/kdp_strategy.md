- Michael Shellenberger, *San Fransicko: Why Progressives Ruin Cities* (2021) — ideological overlap on policy failure thesis; broader audience

- Robert Marbut, *Ending Homelessness* — operational focus; policy professional audience

**Positioning statement**: Between Shellenberger's political polemic and Desmond's academic sociology, The Weaponization of Compassion occupies the evidence-driven center. It grounds its structural critique in peer-reviewed medical and federal policy literature while maintaining the narrative accessibility of trade non-fiction.
