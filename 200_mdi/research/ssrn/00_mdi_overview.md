### Project Identity

The **Material Dignity Infrastructure (MDI)** is a full-spectrum, end-to-end industrial pipeline engineered to permanently eliminate chronic unsheltered homelessness in the Los Angeles metropolitan area. It is the technical successor to the 1908 Los Angeles Aqueduct in scope and structural ambition — a permanent realignment of the metropolitan residential core, not a social service program.

The MDI replaces a legacy system of fragmented municipal services with a single coordinated architecture spanning three operational phases: street-level clinical field engagement, legal lever-facilitated compelled pathways for the treatment-resistant, and a terminal infrastructure node (the converted commercial tower) that delivers ontological permanence to stabilized residents.

**The Governing Principle**: Housing is not the reward for getting better. It is the clinical precondition for getting better at all.

**The Singular Prototype Threshold (SPT)**: Expansion to the sixteen million square foot, twenty-tower network is gated by a binary proof of concept at **One California Plaza** — currently in Chapter 11 receivership following a $300M default, market-verified floor at $120,000,000. Acquisition budget: $197,500,000. Capital recovery: 29 months via $82M annual Efficiency Surplus.

### The Three-Phase Pipeline (At a Glance)

```
PHASE 1: FIELD ARCHITECTURE (Months 1-24, Pre-Tower)
  ├── By-Name HMIS Registry (all encampment individuals named and mapped)
  ├── ACT Team Sustained Presence (psychiatrist / peer / social worker / outreach)
  ├── Legal Lever System (CARE Court / AOT / LPS — parallel to voluntary track)
  └── Warm Offer Window Execution (specific unit + specific anchor at crisis moment)

PHASE 2: INTAKE AND STABILIZATION (Ground Floor)
  ├── Zone A: Sally Port (high-acuity, interlocking doors, CIT + peer)
  ├── Zone B: Triage/Acuity Segmentation (low-acuity flow vs. supervised nodes)
  ├── Metabolic Stabilization Sequence (rehydration → refeeding → psychiatric)
  └── Rapid Clinical Handoff (on-site psychiatric emergency node)

PHASE 3: ONTOLOGICAL PERMANENCE (Residential Tower)
  ├── ALMU: 150 sq ft sovereign cabin — wet bath, murphy bed, induction prep,
  │         perimeter window priority, STC 65, keycard, Stewardship Contract
  ├── Dunbar Verticality: 13 Home Pods × 154 individuals, pod-isolated circulation
  ├── Biophilic Infrastructure: Tier 1 pod alcove (every pod) + Tier 2 sanctuary (every 7 floors)
  ├── Pod Common Kitchen: adjacent to biophilic alcove, social bonding infrastructure
  ├── Social Infrastructure: Pod Steward, Resident Civic Engagement Pathway, species-neutral, coworking
  ├── Cybernetic Grid: Life-safety telemetry only, Privacy Firewall, Gausium robotics
  └── Tenancy Bridge Guarantee: Contract holds until private market lease is verified
```
