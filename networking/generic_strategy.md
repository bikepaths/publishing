# General Strategy for Online Policy Networking

Online discourse contains distinct commentator archetypes. Successful engagement requires calibrating communication to target mechanistic levels, preventing both abstract alienation and superficial debates.

## Archetype Taxonomy and Tactical Engagement

- **Technical Skeptic**: Focuses on narrow engineering metrics, zoning ordinances, and plumbing constraints. This commentator operates at mechanistic or inert levels of understanding.
  - *Counter-play*: Steel-man engineering points before introducing relational layout models that utilize existing commercial wet stacks.
- **Moral Ideologue**: Reduces policy debates to binaries regarding human rights versus enforcement mandates. This category operates at illusory or collective oversimplification levels.
  - *Counter-play*: Depolarize debates using biological facts, showing that both single-occupancy isolation and street sweep trauma trigger identical sympathetic nervous system collapse.
- **System Cynic**: Claims bureaucratic inertia and municipal funding deficits prevent structural changes. This perspective operates at outdated expertise or expert blindness levels.
  - *Counter-play*: Present braided finance blueprints proving how private commercial real estate acquisition and Medi-Cal billing bypass municipal general funds.

## Communication Mandates for Evasion and Impact

- **Syntax Evasion**: Avoid structured, bulleted lists in external comment threads. Write irregular paragraph blocks to bypass automated large language model filters.
- **Socratic Hooking**: Open discussions by querying specific technical limitations or regulatory roadblocks before proposing architectural solutions.
- **Escalation Management**: Reject personal attacks and emotional appeals. Anchor responses in physical layouts and neurological outcomes.
